Brooklin Logan

I was born and raised in Cleveland, Ohio. I was accepted to go to high school at Cleveland School of the Arts, first as a theatre major and then as a creative writing major. When I graduated in 2013, I started my next fall semester as a freshman at Kent State University, where I majored in Teaching English as a Second Language (TESL) and minored in Japanese and in Education. The summer after my fourth year, I was able to study abroad at Nihon University in Tokyo, Japan.

Currently, I am participating in the Cleveland Codes program with Cuyahoga Community College to become a software developer.
